Some of my Favorite Things

Favorite TV Series

The Maxx is a weird, gritty, and surprisingly emotional animated series that feels like a fever dream in the best way. It jumps between reality and a strange “Outback” world, and I love how it mixes dark humor with genuinely thoughtful moments. The art style is bold and unique, and the show never plays it safe.

Favorite Music

The Dead Milkmen are my go-to band when I want smart, sarcastic punk that doesn’t take itself too seriously. Their songs are funny, catchy, and full of attitude, but they also have a sharp way of poking at culture and everyday nonsense. I like that they sound raw and energetic without trying to be polished.
